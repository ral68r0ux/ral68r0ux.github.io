Favorite Fishing Holes

With the weather warming and the long winter with isolation, it is time to get out and enjoy nature. The fishing season may not start until May, but now is the time to get ready. Make sure you have a campsite reservations, available at www.idahostatesman.com. It is also important to know where to go fishing. This can be found at Family Fishing Waters - Southeast Idaho. While at this site you can get your fishing license. Now it is time to get checking all of your equipment and making sure it is in good working order. Nothing is worse than trying to fish with a tangled line and rotten bait. Get out your pole and line to test and make sure all is working. This is a great time to update your equipment with the latest gadgets. Make sure your pick-up some sunscreen, it has been a while since everyone has been out in the sun.
