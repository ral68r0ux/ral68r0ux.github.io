Favorite Hiking Trails

With the weather warming and the long winter with isolation, it is time to get out and enjoy nature. Hiking is a great way to keep in shape and be out in the fresh air. You can do this with family and or friends. It can also be done alone if you really want to.

     

Pull out all of your equipment and make sure everything is in good condition. Replace worn laces, leaking water bottles, or damaged walking sticks. Now that the equipment is taken care of where are you going? All Trails is a web page that be configured by skill level, length of the hike, and other variables, and provide a list of trails available in Eastern Idaho. Pack your snacks and grab your water, stick, and enjoy.
