It was decided to use a monochromatic color scheme. The hex#161370 was the first color chosen and all of the other colors were picked froma list of colors that were in the monochromatic scheme. They were chosen tocomplement and contrast with each other without clashing to bring the feelingof a clear sky, from different times of day, and cloudy skies. The font colorswere chosen for best contrast to help with reading.
