Scenario 1

Tom Knight is planning an ice fishing trip and want to know what the weather will be link and if the lake has frozen over and the ice is thick enough for fishing.

Scenario 2

Holly is panning the major pruning of the berry fields and needs to know if the weather is going to hold for the time she has scheduled everyone to come.

Scenario 3

Tom loves to ride his bike with his children andgrandchildren. While viewing the Bear Facts Weather page he sees a posting onthe community page that the Bear Lake Bike Shoppe is hosting an annual ride aroundBear Lake. Tom starts planning to take his family to participate.

Scenario 4

Holly needs to make sure that her crop of berries to be thebest and look on Bear Facts Weather to see if she need to irrigate or if therewill be enough rain fall to adequately water the berry canes.

Scenario 5

Tom is taking a group of boys for a day hike. He checks BearFacts Weather to see the conditions of the day. The boys will need to bring ajacket, to begin with, but by the afternoon sunscreen will be needed.
